Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof Rice is a popular West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and a blend of spices. It's a flavorful and colorful dish that is enjoyed in many parts of Africa. This recipe provides a step-by-step guide to creating a delicious pot of Jollof Rice that will delight your taste buds.

From the vibrant red color to the aromatic flavors, Jollof Rice is a staple at celebrations and gatherings. Whether you're new to African cuisine or a seasoned pro, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups uncooked long grain rice (couscous or basmati works well)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 chicken or fish fillets (optional), cut into bite-sized pieces

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Stir in the cumin and salt. Toss with the parsley until evenly coated. If using chicken or fish, add them too.
